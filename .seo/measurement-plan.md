# SEO measurement plan

## Business outcomes

- Primary conversion: confirmed appointment lead or qualified phone call
- Assisted conversions: price-page visit, directions and Instagram contact
- Revenue or qualified lead definition:

## Leading indicators

- Crawl/indexation: canonical pages indexed, sitemap coverage and exclusions
- Search visibility: non-branded impressions/clicks for orthodontics and general dentistry in Zaporizhzhia
- Local visibility: GBP calls, directions, website clicks and localized query samples
- Engagement and conversion: phone clicks, verified form submissions and landing-page conversion rate
- AI referrals and citation samples: `utm_source=chatgpt.com` sessions plus a dated fixed prompt set

## Review cadence

- Release regression check: crawl, link, schema and responsive smoke test before every merge
- Weekly review: indexing, errors and lead-path health during launch
- Monthly review: segmented GSC, analytics, GBP and query-set comparison
- Experiment observation window: define per change; do not attribute ranking movement from a single short sample
